## How the bench runs

- The Brain must hold the knowledge these questions reach for — ingested
  as memories before the bench, not planted to match the questions.
- Each question is asked through `recall()` (the real embedding stone in
  the slot, not a toy); the memory holding the answer must appear in the
  top 10 results. Score: 15/15 or the criterion fails.
- **Answer key recorded above**, verbatim from the Owner — never guessed
  by the builder (and rightly so: the suspected answer to Q15 was wrong).
- Before the bench runs, each answer is ingested into the Brain as a real
  memory; the test is that `recall()` surfaces the right memory in the
  top 10 for each question — the builder knowing the key is not the test.
- Runs at harness time, when the Glove holds the real embedder. Re-runs
  fresh after any change, like everything else.
